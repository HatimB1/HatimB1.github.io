Overview

I am an ambitious science student who strives for knowledge and cognition. My interests include implementing research in many domains, typically Electrical Engineering, Electrodynamics, Wave-Matter Interaction, and Modern Physics. I endeavor to develop my acquaintances and abilities so as to invest them in the accentuation of community work. My curiosity fuels my motivation and idiosyncrasy to explore the intricacies of physical phenomena and the profound philosophical implications that arise from the application of this knowledge in our society. I have distinguished myself through research that has merited an h-index of 4, an unusual feat for an undergraduate and indicative of the impact usually seen at the graduate or PhD level.
